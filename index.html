Story Board Signs

The following are signs for the story-board experiment I'm doing at EvergreenHealth in Kirkland. The intent is to tell a story by slowly flipping through these signs in a high-foot-traffic location, ostensibly, on the sidewalk in front of the main entrance to EvergreenHealth.

I want to share information on non-therapeutic forced genital cutting without immediately provoking a defensive reaction on the part of the people receiving the presentation.

I also want to offer people caught up in this issue a way out, lest they simply continue to defend the indefensible.
