Story Board Signs

The following are signs for a story-board experiment I'm doing at EvergreenHealth in Kirkland. The intent is to tell a story by slowly flipping through these signs in a high-foot-traffic location, ostensibly, on the sidewalk in front of the main entrance to EvergreenHealth.

I want to share information on non-therapeutic forced genital cutting without immediately provoking a defensive reaction on the part of the people receiving the presentation.

I also want to offer people caught up in this issue a way out, lest they simply continue to defend the indefensible.

First production run - six signs

To get the best price on signs, I need to do six in a run. This is my first set of six signs. I have reduced the font size a bit to get more text per sign.

I'm looking forward to seeing how these signs work in real life, and will iterate based on what I learn.
